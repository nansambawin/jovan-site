24K KARAT GOLD


24K gold. A 24 Karat gold is 100 per cent pure gold and does not have any other metal mixed.
In the local market, it is known as 99.9 per cent pure and
has a distinct bright yellow color.
The 24 karat gold is more expensive than 22 or 18 Karat gold.

22K KARAT GOLD

22K Gold. The 22 Karat gold is commonly used in making regular jewellery.
22K means that 22 parts of the metal amount to gold and
the rest two parts are some other metals that make the texture of gold harder,
thus making the metal durable. In 22K gold, only 91.67 per cent is pure gold.

18k KARAT GOLD

18K gold is a good choice if you want gold jewelry that's
as pure as possible while still offering some practicality.
However, it's not the best option if you need a ring that's very durable,
or if you're on a limited budget.
